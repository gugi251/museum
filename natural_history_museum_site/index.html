Current Exhibits

Step into a world long gone with "Unearthed Giants," our latest immersive exhibition. Witness life-sized reconstructions of Ice Age mammoths, sabre-toothed cats, and woolly rhinos, brought to life with incredible detail. This exhibit showcases rare fossils unearthed from around the globe, offering a profound glimpse into Earth's most colossal creatures

Our interactive displays let you experience what it's like to be a palaeontologist. Use real excavation tools to uncover fossil replicas, and listen to first-hand accounts from leading experts on their most thrilling discoveries. This is a journey through ancient ecosystems that will ignite your sense of adventure.

Permanent Collection

Journey through millions of years of history in our permanent collection. From the intricate beauty of mineral formations to the awe-inspiring skeletons of ancient sea creatures, our galleries are a celebration of the natural world. Discover meticulously crafted dioramas that recreate historic habitats and explore our vast collection of human artifacts that tell the story of our shared evolutionary path.

This foundational exhibit features hands-on specimens and detailed models, providing an accessible and engaging educational experience for visitors of all ages. It is a deep dive into the very fabric of our planet and the life that has shaped it.

Tours & Talks

Enhance your visit with a guided tour or an insightful lecture. Our expert guides lead you on behind-the-scenes journeys into our archives and labs, revealing the secrets of our collections. Our guest lecture series features talks by renowned biologists, geologists, and conservationists on topics ranging from the mysteries of deep-sea life to the future of biodiversity.

We offer a variety of talks for all interests. Check our website for the full schedule and special events, including our popular "After Dark" lecture series. Advance booking is highly recommended for all events, with most included in your general admission or free for members.

Summer Program for Kids

Our summer program transforms the museum into a playground of discovery! Designed to spark curiosity and creativity in young explorers, our weekly workshops offer hands-on fossil hunts, model-building challenges, and scientific experiments. Each session is a new adventure, supervised by our team of trained and passionate educators.

Open to ages 5-12 with flexible booking options, our program provides a safe and fun environment for children to engage with science through active learning and play. This is where future palaeontologists and naturalists get their start!
